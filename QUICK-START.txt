1️⃣  START-EVERYTHING.bat
    ↳ Starts ngrok + backend + frontend
    ↳ Wait 15 seconds for startup

2️⃣  update-ngrok-urls.bat
    ↳ Auto-configures all files
    ↳ Shows URLs for Google OAuth

3️⃣  Update Google OAuth Console
    ↳ https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials
    ↳ Add the URLs shown in step 2

4️⃣  Close windows and rerun START-EVERYTHING.bat
    ↳ Needed for config changes to take effect

5️⃣  Access your ngrok URL!
    ↳ Works from ANY device, ANYWHERE
